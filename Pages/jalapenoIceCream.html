Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can fruit cocktail, drained well (I use Libby's cherry cherry cherry in light syrup)
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and diced small
  • 2 tablespoons tequila
  • 1.5 cups heavy cream
  • .5 cup Splenda sugar substitute (or sugar)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. To the fruit cocktail, add jalapenos and tequila. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate.
  2. Mix the half and half with the sugar and vanilla. Stir until sugar is dissolve. Place milk/sugar mixture into machine and churn for 15-20 minutes. Add fruit mixture and continue churning until ice cream is formed (about 5 minutes). Scoop into a freezer safe container with lid, cover and freeze for at least 2 hours.
  3. Place milk/sugar mixture into machine and churn for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Add fruit mixture and continue churning until ice cream is formed (about 5 minutes).
  5. Scoop into a freezer safe container with lid, cover and freeze for at least 2 hours.
